注册式表单-风格 block

结构使用:block

<div class="cc_item">

    <label class="form-label">用户名</label>

    <div class="form-input-block">
        <input type="text" name="name" class="required input-text radius"  data-tip="请输入您的姓名" data-valid="isNonEmpty||maxLength:2" data-error="用户名不能为空||长度不能小于2位" placeholder="用户名">
    </div>

</div>

美化元素:radio, checkbox

引入css, 注：只引一个，可取消低版的。http://post.aihuagrp.com/assets/js/libs/cc/CCForm.v2.css

<div class="cc_item">

    <label class="form-label">性别</label>

    <div class="form-input-block">

        <label class="form-status no-icon">
            <input name="sex" type="radio" value="男" checked>               
            <div class="radio-icon"><span>男</span>  </div>
        </label>

        <label class="form-status no-icon">
            <input name="sex" type="radio" value="女">               
            <div class="radio-icon"><span>女</span>  </div>
        </label>

    </div>
    
</div>
